About

SAIREN COLOR ARCHIVE

Sairen Takashima. SAIREN COLOR ARCHIVE is my record of color and words.

The world is complex and full of contradictions. I cut out the “contours” of emotion and memory—through color and structure.

With abstraction at the core, I create works digitally and sometimes project them into fashion, expanding into wearable art.

What matters to me is sincerity over appearance. I create not to decorate, but to choose again.

Here, I archive not only finished pieces, but the process of change itself.
