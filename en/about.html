About

Hi, I’m Sairen Takashima.

SAIREN COLOR ARCHIVE is a place to store and publish my digital artworks.
My works are built around a sense of color that arrives before words.
I layer emotions, memories, silence, and impulses — things that resist easy language — into color strata.

Please start with the works themselves.
If the world extends into your body, you can move to the LOOKBOOK and touch the apparel line (TCDA).
Another expression: transforming art from something you “display” into something you “wear.”

For collaboration / publication / commissions: contact me anytime.
