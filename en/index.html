Hello. I’m Sairen Takashima.
SAIREN COLOR ARCHIVE was created as a place to collect and present my digital artworks.

My work is built around the sensation of color—something that reaches you before language does. I don’t rush toward fixed shapes or “correct answers.” I layer feelings, memories, silence, and impulses into color—until they settle into a form.
