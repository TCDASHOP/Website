About

Sairen Takashima is an artist and founder of an apparel brand that treats clothing as an extension of visual philosophy.

Working with color as structure, not decoration — building a language that can be worn, archived, and re-read.

“A moving canvas — where visual thought becomes wearable form.”

— SAIREN
